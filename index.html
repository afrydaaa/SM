Annotation guideline

Verify the QA pairs produced by GPT-4o — retain, revise, or discard.

For each sample you may retain, revise, or discard the QA pair. Base your decision on the four criteria below, then apply the decision rule. Setiap QA dinilai satu orang; begitu disimpan, keputusan terkunci.

CRITERION 1

Query clarity

The query is specific and unambiguous, targeting a particular topic. It avoids vague or overly general questions.

CRITERION 2

Answer correctness

The answer is factually correct and directly supported by the visual content — no hallucinations. Any calculation must be accurate.

CRITERION 3

Category appropriateness

The question matches its assigned category. Mislabelled or ambiguous categories lead to revision or rejection.

CRITERION 4

Multi-page source correctness

For multi-page queries, every referenced page source is accurate. Applies only when the answer draws on more than one page.

Retain — all criteria met
Revise — minor, fixable issues
Discard — major errors that can't be fixed
